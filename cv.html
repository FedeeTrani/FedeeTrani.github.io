Biography & Curriculum Vitae

I am a 22-year-old Computer Engineer.
I graduated in Computer Engineering in December 2024 from Sapienza University of Rome, where I am currently pursuing a Master’s degree in Cybersecurity.

My passion for technology, computer science, and innovation began in July 2019 during a brief internship at IBM's Rome office.

In September 2019, I participated in the 'ACM WomENcourage Hackathon 2019', held in Rome, where I worked on advanced high-tech solutions related to the theme
'Work-Life Balance & Smart Working: Brilliant Ideas and New Tools for the Industrial Sector',
proposed by Avio Aero and CTNA (Cluster Tecnologico Nazionale Aerospazio).
The technical solution I proposed, which turned out to be the winning one, was based on algorithms and the development of an application that integrates
Smart Planning, Productivity, Connectivity and Inclusion to better balance work and private life digitally.
This experience helped me develop problem-solving skills and enhanced my interest creating innovative solutions for real-world challenges.
