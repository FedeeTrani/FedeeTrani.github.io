Patented and Developed System for Smart Home Security

"HomeSentinel: a Multi-Protocol Honeypot for Smart Home Security" (ENG)

Keywords: Smart Home Security, IoT Security, Multi-Protocol Solution, Proactivity, Plug-and-Play, Active Defense Mechanism

Abstract:
The increasing diffusion of smart home devices has introduced new cybersecurity challenges due to the heterogeneous nature of IoT communication protocols and widespread vulnerabilities.
This paper presents HomeSentinel, a multi-protocol honeypot system designed to proactively protect smart home environments and capture malicious activity through detailed logging.
It emulates and monitor devices using the most common wireless communication stacks in smart homes — ZigBee, Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE), and Wi-Fi — creating a realistic and heterogeneous network to attract and deceive attackers.
The paper also details the system architecture and proposes an experimental evaluation methodology to assess its effectiveness.
HomeSentinel detects and mitigates threats, offering a deployable, plug-and-play security solution that provides practical and accessible protection for modern home networks.
